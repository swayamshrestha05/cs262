Exercise 1.1

i. Interesting and tractable
- An app where Calvin students can post, browse, and join projects across all departments; Computer Science, Engineering, Art, Biology, etc.
- Some of the features would include posting the project with a title, description, skills required, department
- Projects can be sorted by department, skill level, class level (freshman, sophomore)
- Messaging with the post author
- Favorites where you can save projects you are interested in

ii. Wacky app
- An app that converts your ordinary Toyota into a Transformer (like Optimus Prime)
- You can upload a picture of your favorite Transformer into the app and the app will directly connect to the car's operating system
- Your car then gains the abilities and personality of the Transformer
- The command "Autobots, roll out" will make your car transform
